Stickers X Steve

"Never, in a million years, would I have thought of venturing into a small business,let alone a sticker business. After the death of my great grandmother, I looked into getting her wedding photo turned into a Sticker. After receiving it, a spark in me started. Since starting my business in late August, I have experienced highs and lows, however, I'm truly blessed to have friends and family by my side reminding me that I shouldn't give up on anything that I set my mind on. That goes for everyone. Whatever passion you have, though not sure if you should go for it,do it! You have nothing to lose."

- Steven Cifuentes
